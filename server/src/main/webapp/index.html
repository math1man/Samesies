Features Download

Sick of the way other apps take the hilariousness out of meeting new people?

A game of Samesies is simple: after matching with someone, each of you is presented 1 question at a time. Each game is 10 questions long, and you can only see the other person's response once you've posted your own; that’s the fun. After you’re done with a game, you can message or play another round! The fun/awkwardness/hilarity never ends.

Browse Communities and Receive Connections!

Once you’ve created an account, you can browse the community and look at other users.

If you choose the Play Samesies! option you’ll be randomly matched with another user and thrown into a game. If you browse the communities you are a member of, you can tap on a user to connect with them.

You can go to the connections section to check on your potential games! This is where you will see people requesting you and your various games. You can accept connections and start new games by tapping on the requestor's name!

If it says “your turn”, then your partner is waiting for you to respond first. If it says their turn, it's your your partner's turn to respond.

Each question appears one at a time, and the connection won't proceed until both responders have answered.
