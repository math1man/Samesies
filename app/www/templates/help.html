Game Modes
Random: You and your partner are given 10 random questions to answer.
Personal: You and your partner are given each other's personal questions to answer.

Edit Profile: Edit information about you.
Browse Questions: Look through our databases of questions.
You are in an episode!
In each episode, you will be matched with someone in your community, and you will both be given the same question. Once you answer the question, you may view your partner's answer. Then, when you are ready, you can continue to the next question.
After you and your partner complete 5 questions, you will be taken to a free chat.
If at any point you wish to end the episode, simply swipe left for a new episode, or swipe right to return to the main menu.
Edit your Samesies Profile.
Your core profile is all that you need to play Samesies. Update your password, change the alias or profile picture that connections see you as, or move your location.
Your optional profile contains additional details you can choose to share with your friends. Add your real name, your age, your gender, or a short blurb about yourself.
Your personal questions are a list of 5 questions that are used when playing the Personal game mode. You must have all 5 questions filled out to play.
