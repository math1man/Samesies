You have made a connection!
In each connection, you are matched with a partner, and you will both be given the same question. Once you answer the question, you may view your partner's answer. Then, when you are ready, you can continue to the next question.
After you and your partner complete 10 questions, you will be able to freely chat.
If at any point you wish to make a new connection, simply swipe left for a new episode, or swipe right to return to the main menu. This will end your previous connection.
