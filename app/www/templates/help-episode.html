Help
You are in an episode!
In each episode, you are matched with a partner, and you will both be given the same question. Once you answer the question, you may view your partner's answer. Then, when you are ready, you can continue to the next question.
After you and your partner complete 10 questions, you will be taken to a free chat.
If at any point you wish to end the episode, simply swipe left for a new episode, or swipe right to return to the main menu.
