Samesies was conceptualized in October of 2014. The founders were talking about a medium for asking a question where both parties wouldn't know the answer until both of them had responded. One of them mentioned it might be fun in a game format. After a few months of sitting on the idea, they finally got off their lazy asses and got it done.
